# Memo Framework

## What To Reuse

Two outside frameworks are useful inputs, but not complete solutions:

- `tech-earnings-deepdive` provides a strong narrative spine:
  - key forces
  - module-led business deep dive
  - variant view
  - anti-bias / pre-mortem
  - action triggers
- `us-value-investing` provides a compact quality overlay:
  - returns quality
  - balance-sheet safety
  - cash generation quality
  - moat assessment

## What To Replace

Replace or override the following:

- prompt-only arithmetic with local scripts
- tech-only framing when the company is not a tech name
- web-search-only data gathering when phase 1 and phase 2 already produced artifacts
- promotional text
- missing references

## Required Memo Sections

### 1. Executive Summary

Must lead with:

- current price
- weighted target price
- upside / downside
- action zone
- conviction summary

### 2. Key Forces

Identify 1-3 forces that actually drive value over the next 3-5 years.

This should not be a checklist. It should explain:

- what matters most
- why it matters now
- which evidence supports it
- what would falsify it

### 3. Business & Earnings Deep Dive

Use sector-appropriate modules from [sector-module-mapping.md](sector-module-mapping.md).

Cover:

- growth drivers
- margin / cash flow quality
- balance-sheet resilience
- management / governance if material
- accounting quality if material

### 4. Valuation Summary

Must be phase-2 driven, not re-invented.

Include:

- primary method
- secondary method
- weighted target price
- scenario values
- reverse DCF or market-implied expectation

### 5. Variant View

The structure should be:

- market or implied view
- our view
- why the gap exists
- which facts would invalidate our view

Variant view must connect to:

- reverse DCF
- current multiple or implied growth
- model assumptions

### 6. Quality Overlay

This is the institutional replacement for a free-floating “Buffett score.”

Use the script output to present:

- four dimensions
- total score
- rating
- one-paragraph interpretation

### 7. Risks & Pre-Mortem

This section should include:

- 3-5 principal risks
- what failure looks like
- early warning indicators
- what would force a downgrade or exit

### 8. Catalysts & Monitoring

Must include:

- key upcoming events
- the metrics to watch
- green / yellow / red triggers

### 9. Decision Framework

Must tie directly to:

- current price
- action price
- target range
- downside case
- position-size band

### 10. Evidence Sources

List the primary sources used and separate:

- model artifacts
- valuation artifacts
- external factual sources
- external opinion sources

## Writing Standard

- lead with the conclusion
- make disagreement with market expectations explicit
- be concise where the evidence is weak
- be precise where the evidence is strong
- state unresolved diligence gaps directly

# Sector Module Mapping

Use this file to decide which business modules deserve 2-3x the coverage.

## Software / SaaS

Prioritize:

- ARR / revenue growth
- NRR / retention
- gross margin and FCF margin
- sales efficiency and Rule of 40
- pricing power / switching costs

## Semiconductors / Hardware

Prioritize:

- end-market demand
- gross margin and utilization
- backlog / book-to-bill / inventory
- capex intensity
- cycle normalization

## Internet / Marketplaces

Prioritize:

- user / GMV growth
- take rate
- contribution margin
- retention and engagement
- network effects

## Industrials / Capital Goods

Prioritize:

- order book / backlog
- margin progression
- working capital discipline
- capex and ROIC
- cycle exposure

## REITs / Real Estate

Prioritize:

- occupancy / same-store growth
- AFFO / NOI
- cap rate assumptions
- leverage and maturities
- asset quality

## Banks / Insurers

Prioritize:

- NIM / spread economics
- credit quality
- capital ratios
- reserve quality
- sustainable ROE / ROTCE

## Biotech / Pharma

Prioritize:

- pipeline milestones
- probability-adjusted asset value
- cash runway / dilution
- patent cliffs
- partnering / licensing terms

## Energy / Materials

Prioritize:

- realized pricing
- unit cost position
- reserve or asset life
- balance-sheet resilience
- normalized mid-cycle cash flow

## Consumer / Retail

Prioritize:

- comparable sales
- unit economics
- inventory and markdown risk
- brand strength
- cash conversion

## Rule

If a module is directly tied to the 1-3 key forces, it deserves disproportionate coverage.
Do not force all sectors through a tech earnings template.

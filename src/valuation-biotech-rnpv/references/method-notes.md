# Biotech rNPV Method Notes

Use this pack for businesses where the market convention is driven by pipeline probability and
timing, not by a stable operating-company `DCF`.

## Primary Method

Use `rNPV` when:

- value is driven by discrete pipeline assets
- approval probability and launch timing matter more than one steady-state terminal value
- asset economics differ by indication, partner structure, or patent life

## Secondary Methods

Use launch scenarios when:

- timing, clinical success, or peak-sales risk is disputed
- the market narrative is dominated by one or two upcoming readouts
- you need a bear / base / bull framing for expected value

Use cash-runway and dilution analysis when:

- the company has negative cash flow
- additional financing is likely before a major value inflection
- dilution is a real part of the underwriting case

## Focus Areas

- pipeline milestones and readout timing
- stage-normalized probability of success
- peak sales and ramp
- economics structure: owned, royalty, profit share, or partnered
- patent life, exclusivity, and launch timing
- cash runway and financing need

## Common Failure Modes

- using company pipeline marketing language as if it were a probability estimate
- ignoring financing need while presenting a fully diluted pipeline value
- double-counting approved revenue and pipeline value for the same asset
- using third-party pipeline aggregators without verifying against official registries
